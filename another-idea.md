# Another Idea: Browser Time Awareness Extension

A companion (or v2) extension for tracking and visualizing browser usage patterns.

## Core Concept

"I always feel like I'm not too aware of my time on the browser — how long do I stay on a website, do I switch around, do I have music open a lot, etc. Lots of data, but I don't have it captured — I guess the company does, but I don't!"

## Potential Features

- Time spent per domain per day/week
- Tab switching frequency and patterns
- Active vs. idle time detection
- Category grouping (social, work, entertainment, music)
- Visual dashboard (daily/weekly/monthly trends)
- Export data (the user owns their data, unlike the companies)

## Relationship to Holdup

Could integrate with holdup's domain list — e.g., show stats alongside each entry ("you've visited reddit.com 14 times today, avg 8 min per visit"). Or could be a standalone extension entirely.
