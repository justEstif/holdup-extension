Frequently asked questions

What does each mode do?

Overlay shows a popup on top of the page. Fullscreen redirects to a separate interstitial page first.

How does cooldown work?

After you dismiss a nudge, Holdup won't show it again for that domain until the cooldown expires.

  • Until browser restart — lasts until you close the browser
  • For specified minutes — expires after the time you set
  • Always ask — no cooldown, nudge every visit

What does "Redirect URL" do?

Adds a "Go somewhere else" button that takes you to a URL of your choice (e.g. a productive site).

Does Holdup collect any data?

No. Everything stays local in your browser via Chrome's built-in storage. No analytics, no servers.
